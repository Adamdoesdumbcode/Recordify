FAQ

1. How do I record my screen?

Click the "Start Recording" button, select the screen or window you want to share, and click "Share."

2. Why can I only hear my computer audio?

Ensure you select the option to include your microphone when prompted.

3. How do I download the recording?

After stopping the recording, click the "Download Recording" button to save it to your device.
